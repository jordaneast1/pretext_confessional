Variable line widths in userland.

This page does not give the browser one rectangular text box and hope for the best. It lays out one line at a time around a changing cutout shape, using the same prepared paragraph and a different width per row.

This is the missing step after height estimation. Once line boundaries are explicit, a layout can choose its own contour, anchor text against visual elements, and still stay dynamic under resize.
